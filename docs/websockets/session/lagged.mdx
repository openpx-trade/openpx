---
title: "Lagged"
description: "Slow consumer dropped messages."
---

The outbound dispatch channel overflowed: a slow consumer missed deltas.
OpenPX raises this explicitly rather than silently skipping (the way
`tokio::sync::broadcast` would).

<ResponseField name="last_seq" type="u64" />

A `Lagged` event is followed by a [`BookInvalidated`](/websockets/session/book-invalidated)
with `reason: Lag` for every affected market.
